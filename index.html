Miami

Miami is a city in the state of Florida, United States, administratively, Miami is located in Miami-Dade County, and is the largest city in the South Florida metropolitan area. Miami is the county seat of Miami-Dade County, and the largest city in the metropolitan area in South Florida. The city's name is derived from the Miami tribe, an indigenous tribe that once had a large area in the Eastern United States that has now ceased to exist. Miami today is a major settlement for Cuban-Americans.
